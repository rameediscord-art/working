Create a premium, fully functional SaaS-style website for a Discord-based digital services business. The website must look production-ready, trustworthy, and compliant with payment processor onboarding requirements.

The design should feel modern, premium, gaming-tech inspired, and highly professional — similar to a legitimate SaaS startup or digital marketplace.

STYLE & BRANDING

* Dark-themed UI
* Purple + blue neon accent colors
* Glassmorphism panels
* Soft gradients
* Smooth animations and hover effects
* Modern typography
* Minimal but premium layout
* Fully responsive (mobile-first)
* High conversion optimized
* Fast-loading and SEO-friendly

The site should feel trustworthy, polished, and business-ready — NOT like a template or “coming soon” page.

WEBSITE STRUCTURE
=================

1. HERO SECTION

Create a large modern hero section with:

Headline:
“Premium Discord Digital Services & Community Access”

Subheadline:
“Purchase premium digital services, tools, memberships, and community access instantly through our secure Discord-based platform.”

Primary CTA Button:
“Buy Now”

Secondary CTA Button:
“Join Discord”

Include:

* Animated background glow
* Floating UI cards
* Discord-themed visuals
* Premium SaaS-style layout
* Trust badges like:

  * Secure Payments
  * Instant Access
  * 24/7 Support

====================================

2. SERVICES SECTION

Create editable service/product cards.

Each card must include:

* Service title
* Short description
* Pricing
* Features list
* Buy button integrated with Lemon Squeezy checkout
* Optional “Popular” badge

Example services:

* VIP Discord Membership
* Premium Bot Access
* Gaming Coaching
* Marketplace Tools
* Automation Services
* Digital Downloads

Make cards visually premium with hover effects and glass panels.

====================================

3. HOW IT WORKS SECTION

Display a clean 4-step process timeline:

Step 1:
Choose your desired service.

Step 2:
Complete secure payment via Lemon Squeezy.

Step 3:
Join our Discord server.

Step 4:
Automatically receive your role, access, or digital product.

Use animated icons and modern timeline styling.

====================================

4. PRICING SECTION

Create modern pricing cards with:

* Monthly and lifetime plans
* Highlighted “Most Popular” package
* Feature comparisons
* Strong CTA buttons

Example pricing tiers:

* Starter
* Pro
* Elite

Use premium SaaS pricing layout.

====================================

5. FAQ SECTION

Include expandable accordion FAQs.

Questions:

* How do I receive my Discord role?
* How long does delivery take?
* What payment methods are accepted?
* Can I request refunds?
* Is support available after purchase?
* How do subscriptions work?

Generate professional answers automatically.

====================================

Create a fully functional support area.

Include:

* Contact form

* Discord invite button

* Average response time indicator

Example:
“Average response time: under 12 hours”
